About Charleston Beach Club

Your perfect beachside dining experience

Our Story

Charleston Beach Club is a vibrant beachside restaurant located near Tema Harbour. Known for its relaxing atmosphere, fresh seafood, and great music, it has become a favorite spot for both locals and tourists looking to enjoy good food by the ocean.

Our Mission

To deliver a unique dining and relaxation experience by combining delicious meals, refreshing drinks, and a beautiful beach environment. We aim to create unforgettable moments for every guest.
